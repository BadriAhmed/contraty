# Contraty — Concept & Market Analysis

## What Is Contraty?

An AI-powered contract generation platform for Tunisian law, available in Arabic and French. Users fill a short wizard (6-8 questions), the AI generates a legally-grounded contract as a downloadable PDF. Free tier with ads, paid tier without ads and with unlimited generations.

## Market Gap

No one sells downloadable Tunisian legal contract templates to individuals. The market splits into:

| Existing solution | Problem |
|---|---|
| **juristunisie.com** | Free forum, no templates, just user Q&A threads |
| **legal-doctrine.com** | Enterprise SaaS (€€€), legal database/search, not consumer-facing |
| **doctrine.fr** | France only, irrelevant to Tunisian law |
| **Lawyers** | 200-500 TND per contract draft, takes days |
| **Free PDFs on random blogs** | Incomplete, outdated, no Tunisian legal grounding |

Contraty fills the gap: **instant, cheap (free/ad-supported or 30 TND premium), legally-reviewed templates.**

## Target Audience

1. **Auto-entrepreneurs / freelancers** — Need invoices, service contracts, NDAs
2. **Small business owners** — Statuts SARL, partnership agreements
3. **Landlords & tenants** — Baux d'habitation, état des lieux
4. **Employees & HR** — CDI, CDD, rupture conventionnelle
5. **Individuals** — Compromis de vente, prêt entre particuliers, procuration

## Monetization Strategy

| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | 0 TND | 2 AI-generated contracts/month, display ads, watermark on PDF |
| **Unlimited** | 30 TND / month (or 10€) | Unlimited AI contracts, no ads, no watermark, email delivery, contract history |

Revenue channels:
- AdSense (free tier visitors)
- Paddle/Lemon Squeezy subscriptions (international merchant of record — no Tunisian patente needed)
- One-time contract purchase: 5 TND per contract (pay-as-you-go alternative)

Target: 1,000 free users → 50 paid subscribers (5% conversion) → 1,500 TND/month MRR. Conservative but realistic for Tunisia.

## Naming

**Contraty** — French-Arabic portmanteau: "Contrat" (contract) + "تي" (my). Accessible to French-first Tunisians, pronounceable in English, signals personalization.

Domain candidates: `contraty.com`, `contraty.co`, `contraty.app`
